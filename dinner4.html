Pork Sinigang Recipe

A comforting Filipino sour soup made with tender pork, vegetables, and a tamarind-based broth.

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add pork and simmer for 10 minutes, skimming off any scum.
  2. Add onions, tomatoes, and fish sauce. Simmer for another 30-40 minutes until pork is tender.
  3. Stir in tamarind paste or sinigang mix and let it dissolve.
  4. Add radish, eggplant, and chili peppers. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add kangkong (or bok choy) and okra. Cook for another 2 minutes.
